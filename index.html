When I was young, I loved computers, typing, and technology. Being engaged in the medical field as a medical scribe, I was typing constantly and on track to become a Physician Associate. However, I soon realized that the medical field was not fulfilling/rewarding for me. After talking to some friends who were software engineers, they recommended me to try coding and a bootcamp. For the next couple of weeks, I was coding every night after work for 2 hours from FreeCodeCamp and Harvard's CS50 lectures. That was when I realized my vocation and passion to be a software engineer, and I applied to App Academy.

During the 6 months at App Academy, I learned a lot of new technologies/skills such as JavaScript, Python, Express, Sequelize, Flask, React/Redux, HTML, and CSS. Being able to apply these skills to create numerous projects was very fun and an amazing experience for me. In addition, I had the opportunity to meet and work with some amazing people throughout the duration of this program who I learned lots from. I hope to meet more people in the future with whom I can share my experience/skills with and create something amazing in the future!

In my free time I enjoy sight-seeing at the beach, playing games, watching anime/tv shows, or trying new food places!
